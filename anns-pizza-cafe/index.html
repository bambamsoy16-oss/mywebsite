About Ann

Started with One Oven and One Router

Ann opened the shop in 2019 with a single pizza oven, three borrowed laptops, and a 200Mbps connection in the back of a laundromat. Five years later, we've grown into the neighborhood's go-to spot for late-night fuel — whether that's a slice, a study session, or a six-hour gaming marathon.

We stay open 24/7 because someone is always working, always playing, always hungry. The oven never cools and the door never locks.
