About NovaTech

Engineered by Builders, For Builders

We started NovaTech in a Brooklyn garage in 2014 with one goal: deliver custom PCs without the markup or the wait. A decade later, we've shipped over 12,000 systems to gamers, creators, and developers across the country.

Every rig is hand-built, stress-tested for 48 hours, and shipped with the cables labeled. No bloatware. No corner-cutting. Just hardware that works the day it arrives and years after that.
